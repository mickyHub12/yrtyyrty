Order Status Guide

Shipping Window:  Flies by Dozen  = 15-30 Days | Everything Else = 7-10 Days | Courses = Immediate Access Online

  • Flies by the Dozen: If you placed an order for fishing flies by the dozen, you can expect your order in 15-30 days as we hand tie each of our flies to order.
  • Fly Assortments, Boxes & Leader: If you placed an order containing assortments, boxes or leaders, you can expect those to ship in 5-7 days (longer for intl customers)
  • Courses & Memberships:  Courses and Memberships are available immediately after purchase and can be viewed at the top of the My Account page (scroll up)
  • Mixed Orders:  Orders containing both flies by the dozen and other items will ship in split orders along the same times as mentioned above.

** Fishing Flies are tied to order and will take 15-30 days from the order date to ship.  If your fly order is past the 30 day mark, please contact us and tell us. **
